About

Carlos Eduardo Murilho Nogueira

Carlos Eduardo Murilho Nogueira is an independent researcher focused on applied economics, economic history, and public policy evaluation. His work integrates data analysis, historical context, and quantitative modeling to develop insights on development, demographic transitions, institutional change, and macroeconomic dynamics. He publishes independent working papers and research reports on platforms such as Zenodo. The Murilho-Nogueira Institute for Applied Economics is his personal research laboratory, dedicated to rigorous analysis and accessible dissemination of economic knowledge.

  • Independent researcher and founder of the Murilho-Nogueira Institute.
  • Research interests: applied economics, economic history, public policy, development.
  • Outputs: working papers, reproducible datasets, analytical reports.
